Our School

Government Lower Primary School Mulkadu is a government school located in the village of Mulkadu, Karnataka. We provide quality education for children from Class 1 to Class 5, following the Karnataka State Board curriculum.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, inclusive, and quality education to every child in our community, fostering holistic development through academic excellence, creative activities, and moral values.

Our Vision

A school where every child blossoms into a confident, knowledgeable, and responsible citizen, empowered to contribute positively to society.

Facilities

Our school offers well-ventilated classrooms, a library, a playground, mid-day meal facility, clean drinking water, and separate toilet facilities for boys and girls.
