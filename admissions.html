Admissions

Join the GLPS Mulkadu family — admissions open for 2026-27

Who Can Apply?

  • Children aged 5 years and above as of June 1, 2026
  • Children from Mulkadu and nearby villages
  • All communities and backgrounds are welcome

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Aadhaar card of child and parent
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)

How to Apply

  1. Visit the school office during working hours
  2. Fill out the admission form with complete details
  3. Submit the required documents
  4. Complete the registration fee payment
  5. You will receive an admission confirmation letter

Important Dates

  • Admission Period: January - March, 2026
  • Class Commencement: June 1, 2026
