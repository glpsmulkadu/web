Admissions

Join the GLPS Mulkadu family — admissions open for 2025-26

Who Can Apply?

  • Children aged 5 years and above as of June 1, 2025
  • Children from Mulkadu and nearby villages
  • All communities and backgrounds are welcome

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Aadhaar card of child and parent
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • 2 passport-size photographs of the child
  • Ration Card (photocopy)
  • Bhagyalakshmi Bond (if applicable)

Important Dates

  • Admission Form Distribution: From April 15, 2026
  • Last Date for Submission: NA
  • School Reopens: June 1, 2026

Fee Structure

GLPS Mulkadu is a government school. Education is completely FREE for all students. No tuition fees are charged. Mid-day meals, textbooks, and uniforms are provided free of cost.
